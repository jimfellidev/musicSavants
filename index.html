Services

Music Savants offers a nearly endless supply of music lessons to you or your loved ones, all available at your fingertips by clicking here. Whether you are interested in taking a more traditional route with the piano or violin, or want to get in touch with your inner rockstar with the electric guitar, Music Savants will accomodate all! If you don't have access to our store, we will travel to you! Simply contact us! Pricing varies based on location and instrument.

After contacting our staff, we will ask you a small list of questions to ensure we match you with the teacher best fitted to your needs and personality.

About

Established in 2012, Music Savants has been connecting students with teachers regularly for over seven years. We recently opened our first store, located at 101 Makebelieve ln, Sewell NJ, 08080. Feel free to stop in and discover what instrument is best for you!

Music Savants was first developed when its owner, Todd Tomato, repeatedly attempted to find cello lessons for his daughter. After wiggling his schedule repeatedly in order to get her to class, he realized there had to be a better way. Now there is!
